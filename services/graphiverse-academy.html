Track 01

Advanced Data Architecture & Handling

Build reliable, clean data foundations. Learn to tame messy real-world inputs and keep your models accurate and current.

Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need prior experience to enroll?

Basic Excel familiarity is helpful, but no advanced skills are required for Track 01. During your discovery call we'll assess your level and find the right entry point. Track 02 pairs best with some formula experience.

Can I take both tracks together?

Yes — and we encourage it. Track 01 teaches you to architect reliable data; Track 02 teaches you to model and visualize it. Together they cover the entire professional analytics workflow.

Are sessions live or self-paced?

All training is delivered in live, personalized sessions tailored to your schedule. You get direct expert feedback and real-time Q&A — far more effective than pre-recorded courses.

How is pricing determined?

Every engagement is quoted individually based on your goals, session count, and chosen tracks. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote. Payment is 50% upfront and 50% upon completion.

What support is available after training?

All Academy students receive post-training support: file reviews, formula checks, and expert Q&A responded to within 24 hours. We remain invested in your success after the last session.
